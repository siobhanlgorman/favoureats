A Note About Us

This website came was inspired by my book club friends who wanted a website where we could easily share the recipes enjoyed during our regular book (and cook) club evenings. We've been meeting and eating for nearly 20 years so there's a lot of recipes!

In the last couple of years when no one was able to eat out, we started sharing recipe ideas to feed the family. So this website is an amalgamation of our gourmet dinner recipes and family-friendly weekday dinner recipes

The Favoureats website is a means to store and share your favourite recipes - everything in one place.

All the recipes are vegetarian or fish as that's what we enjoy and we hope you do too!

Please share your thoughts on our recipes in the reviews section!

Siobhan
