About Us

This site came about as we wanted a place where we could easily share the recipes used during our regular book (and cook) club evenings.

Then came lockdown and we wanted also to share our favourite 'feed the family easily' recipes

So this website is an amalgamation of our gourmet dinner recipes and family-friendly weekday dinner recipes

We also wanted the ability for users to store their own recipes in one place where they could edit and delete them if they wished

All the recipes are vegetarian or fish as a few of the group don't eat meat.

We hope you enjoy the recipes and please share your thoughts on them in the reviews section!
